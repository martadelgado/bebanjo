And what are some of my interests? So, my main interest is Javascript but that’s such a general topic I’ve decided to narrow it down a bit so you can understand what I like and have to offer. These are all topics I have some experience with and what I am most interested diving deeper into:

I wish I would have worked with this sooner as it’s a huge life saver! This has changed my mindset when writing new code and has served as a gateway for other front-end testing. Next up, I plan to learn integration and unit testing.
